WELCOME TO LUX TENEBRIS.

RECENT UPDATE:
ROGUE'S GALLERY

Hey everyone! So, update on the progress of the Rogue's Gallery world. Because of university projects, some characters may be getting reworks or redesigns soon! The first of those is Vulcan, who is being fused with the concept for Warlord. The two are very similar, and because of how non-fleshed out Warlord was, I felt that the best case of action was to fuse the two to create a stronger concept.

Secondly, design work for Azi Martin may finally begin soon! The concept for her is there, and there are several planned scenes with her and Fox, so I felt like she should finally get her own design.

Lastly, Since the ending of the Outcasts storyline, Eden needed a new home. So, I've placed her in this universe, with some more fleshed out details, like a full name (Eden Valdez), new hero name (LIGHTSTRIKE), and new position in The Guild!

FEATURED CHARACTERS SHOWCASE

"MITZI"

A deep sea Saphretean, thrust into a position of immense power because of a plan gone wrong and a relic of godlike power, Mitzi has a very chaotic life. Luckily, her and her new pirate crew seem to be able to handle it. Somewhat.

"LIGHSTRIKE"

Eden Valdez, aka Lighstrike, used to be a hero. That was before her home dimension was ripped to shreads. Having been saved by a Glitch and placed in another world, she has been working as an assassin ever since, while trying to find out if her old friends are still out there somewhere.

GUEST CHARACTER: "V"

Created by Nicebur.

V had a life before this. Before the darkness that clouds their vision, the memories barely clung to, and the hatred that burns slowly at their soul. The Glitch attempted to save them before, but are they even possible to save now?

"WATCHER ZERO"

PROTOCOL: ELIMINATE.
Watcher Zero, like the other Watchers, used to be a force to keep the universe stable, but they now have a new prime directive: to hunt down Nyx, eliminate her, and return the universe to their ideal state of order.

FEATURED STORIES SHOWCASE

ROGUE SIGNAL

The story of a dimensional glitch, never meant to exist in the first place, the dark force that hunts her down, and a world-breaking second apocalypse on the horizon. Oh, and the occasional message floating on a rogue radio signal.

BRASS OWLS

Life in the capital city of Bizment can be rocky, but they'll make it through, together. This is the story of a group of outcasts, rejects, and thieves, all from their own difficult pasts, and the found family they have formed together.

TEAM SPECTRUM

Who would've thought, when you put 3 genius inventors, two aliens, and a cybernetically enhanced amputee soldier in a superhero team together, you get a suprisingly effective team. Fighting villains, family members, university life, and interdimensional threats, life is quite the adventure.

BROKEN ISLANDER PIRATES

A crew of misfit pirates, and the godlike fish they keep onboard. Life isn't just smooth sailing though. A dark force manifests somewhere in the deep, and nobody will be safe from the waves
